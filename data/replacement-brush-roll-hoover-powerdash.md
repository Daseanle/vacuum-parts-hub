## Installation Guide

Replacing your Hoover PowerDash brush roll is simple:

1. **Power off** and unplug the vacuum
2. **Locate** the brush roll compartment (usually marked)
3. **Remove** the old brush roll (may require a quarter-turn or clip release)
4. **Insert** the new brush roll until it clicks
5. **Test** the vacuum to ensure proper fit

## Maintenance Tips

To extend the life of your new brush roll:
- Clean regularly (every 1-2 weeks)
- Replace when performance drops (typically 6-12 months)
- Store vacuum in a dry place
- Avoid vacuuming large debris that could damage the brush roll

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How often should I replace my brush roll?**
A: For Hoover PowerDash, we recommend every 6-12 months depending on usage.

**Q: Will an aftermarket brush roll void my warranty?**
A: Generally no, but check your warranty terms. The Magnuson-Moss Warranty Act protects your right to use aftermarket parts.

**Q: Can I clean and reuse my brush roll?**
A: Some brush rolls are washable, others are not. Check the manufacturer's instructions.
