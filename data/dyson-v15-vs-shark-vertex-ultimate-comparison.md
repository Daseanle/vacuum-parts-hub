**Shark Vertex:**

### Test 2: Hardwood Floor Performance

**Setup:** Fine dust, rice, and Cheerios on 200 sq ft hardwood

**Dyson V15:**
- **Pickup Rate:** 98% (laser helps spot hidden dust)
- **Scattering:** Minimal (fluffy roller design)
- **Edge Cleaning:** Excellent (within 1/8 inch of baseboards)

**Shark Vertex:**
- **Pickup Rate:** 96%
- **Scattering:** Moderate (some rice bouncing)

**Verdict:** Dyson's laser is a game-changer for hardwood. You'll see dust you never knew existed.

---

### Test 3: Pet Hair Challenge

**Setup:** 2 oz of golden retriever hair on carpet and upholstery

**Dyson V15:**
- **Carpet:** 97% pickup, no tangling
- **Upholstery:** 95% with mini motorized tool
- **Hair Wrap:** Zero (anti-tangle comb works perfectly)

**Shark Vertex:**
- **Carpet:** 94% pickup, minor tangling
- **Upholstery:** 92% with pet multi-tool
- **Hair Wrap:** Minimal (self-cleaning brush roll)

**Verdict:** Both excel with pet hair, but Dyson's anti-tangle tech is slightly superior.

## Real-World User Experience (6-Month Test)

### Dyson V15: The Pros

1. **Laser Detection is Addictive**
   - You'll become obsessed with seeing every speck of dust
   - Especially useful for hardwood and tile floors
   - Makes you realize how much traditional vacuums miss

2. **Filtration is Medical-Grade**
   - Noticeable reduction in dust allergies (tested with air quality monitor)
   - Sealed system means zero dust leakage
   - Perfect for asthma sufferers

3. **LCD Screen is Surprisingly Useful**
   - Real-time particle count (satisfying to watch it drop to zero)
   - Battery life indicator (accurate to the minute)
   - Blockage alerts (saves troubleshooting time)

4. **Build Quality Feels Premium**
   - Solid metal components
   - Precise engineering (everything clicks into place)
   - Feels like it'll last 10+ years

### Dyson V15: The Cons

1. **Price is Steep**
   - $749 MSRP (though often on sale for $599)
   - Replacement filters: $60/year
   - Feels like a luxury purchase, not a necessity

2. **Small Dustbin**
   - 0.2 gallons means frequent emptying (2-3 times per whole-house clean)
   - Annoying for large homes or heavy shedders

3. **Max Mode Battery is Pathetic**
   - 10 minutes is barely enough for one room
   - You'll rarely use it (too loud and drains too fast)

1. **Value is Unbeatable**
   - $449 MSRP (often $349 on sale)
   - 90% of Dyson's performance at 60% of the price
   - Best bang-for-buck in the cordless category

2. **Larger Dustbin**
   - 0.35 gallons = fewer trips to the trash

   - 15 minutes vs Dyson's 10 minutes

3. **Slightly Lower Suction**
   - Noticeable on deep carpet pile
   - Requires more passes for embedded dirt
