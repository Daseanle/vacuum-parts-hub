## Installation Guide

Replacing your Roomba s9+ hose is simple:

1. **Power off** and unplug the vacuum
2. **Locate** the hose compartment (usually marked)
3. **Remove** the old hose (may require a quarter-turn or clip release)
4. **Insert** the new hose until it clicks
5. **Test** the vacuum to ensure proper fit

## Maintenance Tips

To extend the life of your new hose:
- Clean regularly (every 1-2 weeks)
- Replace when performance drops (typically 6-12 months)
- Store vacuum in a dry place
- Avoid vacuuming large debris that could damage the hose

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How often should I replace my hose?**
A: For Roomba s9+, we recommend every 6-12 months depending on usage.

**Q: Will an aftermarket hose void my warranty?**
A: Generally no, but check your warranty terms. The Magnuson-Moss Warranty Act protects your right to use aftermarket parts.

**Q: Can I clean and reuse my hose?**
A: Some hoses are washable, others are not. Check the manufacturer's instructions.
