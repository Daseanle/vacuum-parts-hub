## Performance Analysis

### Shark AI Robot
The Shark AI Robot is known for its powerful suction and long battery life. It's particularly effective on both carpets and hard floors, making it a versatile choice for most households.

**Pros:**
- Superior suction power
- Longer runtime
- Advanced filtration system
- Lightweight design

**Cons:**
- Higher price point
- Replacement parts can be expensive

### Dyson Ball Animal 2
The Dyson Ball Animal 2 offers excellent value for money. While it may not match the Shark in raw power, it's more than capable for everyday cleaning tasks.

**Pros:**
- More affordable
- Good performance on hard floors
- Easy to maintain
- Readily available parts

**Cons:**
- Shorter battery life
- Slightly heavier

## Which Should You Choose?

**Choose Shark AI Robot if:**
- You have a large home with multiple floor types
- You want the best performance regardless of price
- You value longer cleaning sessions

**Choose Dyson Ball Animal 2 if:**
- You're on a budget
- You have a smaller living space
- You prioritize value over premium features
